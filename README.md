# Work Calendar

A simple work calendar for tracking shifts, hours, and companies without using cloud storage.

Everything is stored locally in your browser, giving you full control of your data.

## Features

- 📅 Simple yearly calendar
- 🏢 Track work across multiple companies
- ⏰ Add start and finish times
- 📊 Automatic hour calculations
- 🎨 Different colours for each company
- 🔍 Filter by company
- 📈 Total hours summary
- 📤 Export data to Excel
- 📥 Import data from Excel
- 💾 Local browser storage (no accounts or cloud)
- 📱 Mobile-friendly design

## Why this app?

This app was built for people who work for multiple employers and need a quick way to record their work without complicated software.

Examples include:

- Agency workers
- Casual employees
- Contractors
- Freelancers
- Healthcare workers
- Support workers
- Cleaners
- Drivers
- Hospitality staff

## Staff Shift Planning

The calendar can also be used as a simple staff shift planner.

Managers can:

- Add employee shifts
- Schedule staff work
- View shifts by company
- Keep track of working hours
- Export schedules to Excel
- Import schedules when needed

## Data Storage

Your data stays on your device.

- No account required
- No cloud storage
- No subscriptions
- No tracking
- No internet connection required after loading the app

All information is saved using your browser's Local Storage.

## Install as a Mobile App

You can install Work Calendar on your phone so it works like a normal app.

### Android (Chrome)

1. Open the app in Google Chrome.
2. Tap the **⋮** (three-dot menu).
3. Tap **Add to Home screen** or **Install app**.
4. Choose a name if prompted.
5. Tap **Install**.

The app will appear on your home screen and can be opened without using the browser.

### iPhone & iPad (Safari)

1. Open the app in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button.
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Tap **Add**.

The app will now appear on your Home Screen like any other app.
